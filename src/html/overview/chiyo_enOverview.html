What is
Mucomi VR
VTuber Song Ranking?

Mucomi VR is a radio show dedicated to VTubers that is broadcast on Japanese terrestrial TV and YouTube.
It is broadcast every Sunday night at 10:30pm on YouTube, and at 11:30pm on both YouTube and terrestrial TV.

A popular feature of this show is a section where original songs by VTubers are ranked by voting on social media under various themes.
This time, the voting period is from September 17th to October 30th, and the ranking is for all VTubers with original songs without a theme.
In the interim announcement the other day, Chiyoura Chomi's "Butterfly Dream" was ranked second.

Fans who like Chiyoura Chomi and Butterfly Dream are asked to vote.

To get first place, we need support from fans all over the world!
